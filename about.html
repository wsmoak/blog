I'm a member of The Apache Software Foundation where I have spent countless hours answering questions, writing documentation, and managing releases for build automation tools Maven, Continuum and Archiva as well as web application frameworks Struts, Shale and MyFaces.

I have a strong accounting background with payments industry experience, and have also worked with systems in university/non-profit fundraising and healthcare.

I can't pass up an interesting question and will go above and beyond to find an answer. I am passionate about never solving the same problem twice, and I'm a compulsive editor and how-to writer. My first contribution to any project is likely to be a documentation patch, followed by a 'getting started' how-to article.

I can usually be found on Freenode IRC as wsmoak. Lately I've been spending time in

  • #stripe, answering questions about the best way to accept payments online,
  • #clojure, learning about a dynamic programming language that targets the Java Virtual Machine,
  • #openmrs, making things better for new potential contributors to a life-saving application, and
  • #mongodb, #angularjs and #nodejs, working on Nivens, an application to manage rabbitry data.

In addition to the posts here, the Recent Changes page of my wiki will give you a hint about what I've been working on.

I live on a farm in Georgia, where I enjoy vegetable gardening, raising chickens and rabbits, and horseback riding.
